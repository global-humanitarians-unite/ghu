About Global Humanitarians Unite

Who we are

In every nation, humanitarians — who share a passion to help others — strive to improve the world we live in. Global Humanitarians Unite believes that uniting these people would make a world of peace possible. Our mission is to assist in the development and expansion of humanitarian efforts through networking and the creation of a collaborative space.

What we do

We support humanitarian organizations around the world. By providing online services, we aid fellow humanitarians in gathering resources and coordinate large-scale efforts that involve our member organizations. Our membership fees and donations from global partners are used in funding these efforts. We continuously form alliances that allow us to provide an abundance of resources to humanitarians who are already working to make a difference.

Where we work

We are based in Atlanta, USA, but connect worldwide.
